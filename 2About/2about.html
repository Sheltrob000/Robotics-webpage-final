The Digital Goats are the official FRC Robotics team for Warren Central High School and the Walker Career Center. We have been competeing in competition since 2002. In our most recent year, we were finalists in our two District events for the season, and made a playoff run in state. We also had enough points to qualify us for the FIRST ROBOTICS world championship in Houston, Texas.
